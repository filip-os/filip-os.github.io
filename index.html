About me

Welcome! I am Filippos. I am a Ph.D. student at the California Institute of Technology, department of Mechanical and Civil Engineering, working with professors Thomas Heaton and Monica Kohler. My current ongoing research is centered around developing a damage identification framework and software for civil infrastructure with emphasis on high-rise buildings. We are developing a model updating framework and the software for structural FEM models using dense seismic array measurements.

I’m interested in developing parameter estimation algorithms, in order to update parameterized physics-based models, learning from the data.


You can find out more about specific projects in more detail at the /Projects page.
