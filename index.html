About me

Welcome! I am Filippos. I am a Ph.D. student at the California Institute of Technology, department of Mechanical and Civil Engineering, working with professors Thomas Heaton and Monica Kohler. My Ph.D. research is centered around developing a model updating framework and software for structural FEM models. The goal is damage identification for civil infrastructure with emphasis on high-rise buildings using dense seismic array measurements. The most recent project that I have been working on, as a part of Caltech's Community Seismic Network (CSN), concerns data processing and analysis following the Ridgecrest 2019 Earthquakes.

I’m interested in developing parameter estimation algorithms, in order to update parameterized physics-based models, learning from the data.


Recent projects and teaching are presented in more detail at the /Research page.
