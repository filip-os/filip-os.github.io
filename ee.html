Welcome to the easter egg page!


This is the easter-egg page.

The original idea for the theme/design of the whole website was movies like Blade Runner (1982) (imagine vibrant colors and a retrowave track playing in every page). However, in the end I decided to settle with a more vanilla console theme, inspired by the Linux console. This easter egg page serves as a reminder of this transition.

In addition to that the easter-egg provides a place for experiment with different html elements and test new ideas in different browsers and devices.


<- Here is a stop music button

A simple enough concept but apparently against the design philosophy of most 2000's blog pages.

Note to reader and to self: Google chrome has been apparently following an anti-autoplay policy since 2017 (Google Chrome Autoplay Policy Changes Page). Not knowing that, I was trying to figure out why the music track was not playing, in a vain attempt to replicate the obnoxious loud websites of my youth. After a while I realized that the music was autoplaying just fine in other browsers like MS Edge and IE Explorer. The magic (if you are using Google Chrome and actually clicked somewhere on the page) behind the current autoplaying you might have witnessed in this page is a script that presses the play-music-button as soon as the user clicks anywhere on the page.
